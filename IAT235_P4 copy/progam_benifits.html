PROGRAM BENEFITS

School Active Travel Planning has many benefits for students, the community, and the environment, including:

  1. Increasing physical activity among students, leading to healthier, happier, and more alert learners
  2. Creating opportunities to engage with neighbours and socialize on the way to school
  3. Reducing car travel to school, in turn decreasing traffic congestion, greenhouse gas emissions, and air pollution
  4. Inspiring active travel habits early in life to carry into adulthood
