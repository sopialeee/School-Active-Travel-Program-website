SCHOOL ACTIVE TRAVEL PROGRAM

The School Active Travel Planning program encourages and promotes walking, cycling, and rolling to school.

We consult with school communities and other stakeholders to identify school transportation challenges and opportunities.

Our focus is to: - Encourage more children and their families to walk, cycle, and roll to school - Improve walking and cycling infrastructure around schools - Increase education and awareness around active transportation

The program is a partnership between the City of Vancouver and Vancouver School Board, and is a direct outcome of our Transportation 2040 Plan. The program also supports our Climate Emergency Action Plan targets.
